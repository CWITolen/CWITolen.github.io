The Ferrari F2004

The Ferrari F2004 cemented Michael Schumacher’s legacy and closed out a golden era for Ferrari, marking the end of Ferrari’s 5-year dominance from 2000–2004. Sporting a 3.0L V10 engine, it won 15 out of 18 Grand Prix, 12 pole positions, and 14 fastest laps. Helping Michael Schumacher win his record 7th World Driver's Championship. Still revered today by fans and engineers alike — and often considered one of the greatest F1 cars of all time.
